About the Painting

Vincent van Gogh painted Starry Night in 1889 while at the Saint-Paul-de-Mausole asylum in France.

The swirling sky, bold colors, and expressive brushwork reflect both his emotional turmoil and genius.

Starry Night remains one of the most famous and influential paintings in modern art.
