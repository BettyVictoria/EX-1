⏳ The Persistence of Memory

About the Painting

Painted by Salvador Dalí in 1931, it is a surrealist masterpiece showing melting clocks in a dreamlike landscape.

The work explores the unreliability and fluidity of time, challenging conventional perception of reality.

It remains iconic in art history and is widely studied for its imaginative and symbolic content.
